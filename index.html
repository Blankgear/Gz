1.   Scope of Service

The Service Provider agrees to:

  • Collect the Client's passport from the nominated Embassy or authority upon confirmation that it is ready for collection.
  • Deliver the passport to the Client via Australia Post Express Post with tracking and signature confirmation, or via hand delivery if mutually agreed in writing prior to collection.
  • Provide the Client with a tracking reference number once the passport has been lodged with the courier service.
2.   Nature of Service
  • GZ Delivery Assistance is a wholly owned subsidiary of Global Zeno and is not an agent, employee, or representative of any Embassy, the Government of Nepal, or any Australian government authority.
  • The service is strictly limited to the collection and delivery of the Client's passport. GZ Delivery Assistance will not copy, photograph, or scan any passport or personal documentation.
  • All handling will be conducted with reasonable care and in accordance with applicable Australian Consumer Law.
3.   Fees
Service Fee AUD 50 – 60 per passport
Postage / Courier Cost Paid separately by the Client, or included if agreed prior to service
Payment Terms Fees are payable in full before collection
4.   Liability
  • GZ Delivery Assistance is not responsible for delays caused by the Embassy of Nepal or any other issuing authority.
  • Once the passport is lodged with Australia Post or an approved courier, liability is limited to the terms and coverage provided by that courier service.
  • GZ Delivery Assistance is not liable for loss or damage beyond the postal service's compensation limits.
  • Any damaged, missing, or undelivered passport must be reported to GZ Delivery Assistance within 48 hours of the scheduled delivery date.
5.   Privacy & Handling
  • GZ Delivery Assistance will not copy, photograph, or retain any passport details or personal information beyond what is strictly necessary to complete the delivery.
  • All personal information is handled in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and Global Zeno's Privacy Policy.
  • Passports will be dispatched on the same business day or next business day after collection, where reasonably possible.
